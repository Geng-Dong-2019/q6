23  ! STORAGE SPECIFICATIONS FOR Q
24  ! Change according to

28  ! Max number of atoms is limited only by the size of the variables used as indices
29  ! to the atom arrays.
30  ! Here we use 16-bit signed integers for indexes to the atom arrays
