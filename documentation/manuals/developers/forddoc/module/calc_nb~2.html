Invoked from Qcalc to calculate nonbonded interactions between two masks defined by user.

Make pair lists of interacting atoms in mask1 and mask2

Add new calculation of nonbonded interactions between two sets of residues, e.g. protein aa and q-atoms. The interactions will be calculated for for the first group one at the time against all residues in the second group.

Step through one residue at the time and calculate nonbonded interactions with all q-atoms.

Print final averages and clean up.
